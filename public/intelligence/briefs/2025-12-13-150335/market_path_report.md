_Shift demand earlier in the season without starting a markdown war_

That pattern repeats across cooperative partner-funded pilots and store-as-studio media tests. The practical effect is more visits and better buyer quality in the first half of the holiday period, not just during peak discount days. This makes the Nov 29 to Dec 13 window high-leverage for capturing customers before price noise arrives.

Keep the test tight and pace spend by signal rather than sentiment.
