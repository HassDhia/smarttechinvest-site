Risk Radar

Statistical noise from short holiday windows
View trigger, detection, mitigation

Trigger: Very short test windows (brand Nov 21â€“Dec 12, retailer Nov 21â€“Dec 7) during high-season volatility; staggered/pop-up launches that confound baseline comparisons and inflate apparent lift.

Detection: Instrument daily and hourly conversion and new-enrollment rates, tag cohorts by launch date, run A/A checks and compute confidence intervals and minimum detectable effect (MDE) before concluding; monitor sudden shifts vs pre-holiday baseline and per-cohort variance.

Mitigation: Operator move: pre-calc power/MDE and adjust traffic or extend test where needed; stagger launches and include short-duration controls; treat early signals as directional and run rapid follow-up micro-tests to validate; report effect sizes with CI and hold decisions until statistically defensible.
Technical failure of instant digital reward / loyalty integration
View trigger, detection, mitigation

Trigger: Integration bugs or capacity issues in checkout/SMS/loyalty APIs (failed loyalty account creation, undelivered SMS coupon, broken redirect), causing low enrollment, redemption, or checkout abandonment during pop-up.

Detection: Instrument API success/error rates, SMS delivery and bounce rates, loyalty account creation success rate, checkout abandonment rate at reward step, server/log alerts, and end-to-end synthetic transactions during rollout.

Mitigation: Operator move: run canary rollouts and smoke tests, enable automatic rollback on error thresholds, provide manual fallback (on-site staff-assisted enrollment or printed coupon) during pop-ups, maintain 24/7 operational triage for test window, and instrument SLA-based alerts.
Negative unit economics from BNPL and cash-back
View trigger, detection, mitigation

Trigger: Higher BNPL partner fees and cash-back redemption costs combined with increased redemptions or cannibalization of full-price purchases that erode net contribution despite higher sign-ups.

Detection: Instrument net contribution per transaction segmented by payment method and promotion (gross margin minus BNPL fees and cash-back costs), track redemption rate, repeat-purchase rate, return rates, and cohort-level LTV over 30/90 days.

Mitigation: Operator move: enforce profitability guardrails (pre-set minimum net contribution), negotiate partner fees, cap cash-back or tie to minimum purchase/enrollment thresholds, A/B test reward levels vs conversion, and pause or re-price offers if net contribution falls below threshold.
Privacy / deliverability & consent failure for SMS-based rewards
View trigger, detection, mitigation

Trigger: Insufficient consent capture, regional regulatory constraints, carrier filtering or high unsubscribe/complaint rates that block SMS coupon delivery and prevent onboarding.

Detection: Instrument consent capture rate at checkout, opt-in vs opt-out ratios, SMS deliverability/delivery latency, unsubscribe and complaint metrics, and geolocation-wise delivery failures.

Mitigation: Operator move: enforce clear consent UX and double opt-in where required, add email or in-app fallback channels, segment campaigns by region/consent status, obtain legal sign-off before launch, and pause SMS flows if complaint/delivery thresholds exceeded.
